Currently Working On

M.O.R.Z. Purge

M.O.R.Z. Purge is DOOM-style first person shooter that takes place on the alien plant Zorapterra. As you make your way through the trecherous caves and abandoned facilities, you'll need to deal with the space-inhabiting insectoids that drove out the people before you. Find ammo, weapons, and upgrades - then shoot 'em up!

This game is part of an assignment for my CIT324 class. I am working on it alongside a team of six other people. You can find the project's repository here!
